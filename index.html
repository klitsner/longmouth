shipping & returns

Effective Date: June 18, 2025 Company: Long Mouth Collectibles ApS

Shipping
We ship globally from both the EU and U.S. warehouses. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location and will be shown at checkout. We do not currently ship to the following countries: Cuba, Iran, Indonesia, North Korea, Syria, Ukraine, Russia, Venezuela, and Belarus.

Order Processing
Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. You will receive a tracking link once your order has shipped.

Order Changes & Cancellations
You may cancel or modify your order before it ships by emailing us at emails@longmouth.world with your order number and request.

Returns
We accept returns for unopened and unused products within 14 days of delivery.
- Items must be in original packaging, undamaged, and unused.
- To initiate a return, email emails@longmouth.world with your order number and reason for return.
- Customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
- We reserve the right to refuse refunds for items that are returned damaged or show signs of use.
- Sale items or limited edition items may not be returnable unless defective.

If your return is approved, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned goods.

Lost or Damaged Packages
We are not responsible for items lost in transit. If your package arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of delivery at emails@longmouth.world with your order number and photos of the item.

Refunds
Refunds are issued only for eligible returned items. Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Return Form
While we do not provide prepaid return labels, we recommend including your order number inside the package to help us process your return efficiently.
